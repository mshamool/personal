Football.

Football, also known as soccer, is a team sport played between two teams of eleven players. The objective is to score more goals than the opposing team by maneuvering the ball into their net using any part of their bodies except their hands (except for the goalkeeper). It's a globally loved sport, known for its fast-paced action, skillful players, and passionate fans.

Cricket.

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players. The game revolves around batting, bowling, and fielding. The batting team aims to score runs by hitting the ball bowled by the opposing team, while the bowling team tries to dismiss the batsmen and restrict the scoring. Cricket is a popular sport in many countries, particularly in the Commonwealth nations like India, England, Australia, and Pakista.
